What problem are we solving in the IT industry?

At CoEdify, we are trying to bridge the gap between the employer and the employees they hire. We act as a middleware in order to provide the top talent that is required by an organization and a good rewarding job that an employee aspires for.

We make sure that aspirants who join our tech program go through an intensive industry standard curriculum that helps the aspirants to become software engineers instead of normal software developers. We make sure that all round development is achieved by the aspirants be it honing their coding skills, communication skills or even building up their confidence.

How are we different from others?

We make sure that you get a headstart for your successful career no matter when you start. Period.

We don't focus on conventional training methods that are out there in the market. We believe everyone has their own requirements in order to excel in their career. Everyone is different. So at CoEdify we try to work on root problems of every individual and work our way up instead of imposing a training program. Got great communication skills but lack technical knowledge? Worry not! Frequently Asked Questions

Still doubtful? We got you covered here.

  • What is the eligibility for this program?

    Anyone who has a technical background or has 1+ year of experience can enroll.

  • Why CoEdify ?

    We at CoEdify are trying to bridge the gap between employers and  prospective employees. The training methodology includes not just skill enhancement but also helps in soft skills. Group discussion, mock interviews, grilling coding sessions ensure that our candidates walk out of CoEdify as  a confident IT professional.

  • What is the duration of the course?

    Minimum 3 months extended to 6 months depending upon the roadmap designed by the mentor after profile assessment.

  • What is the mode of training?

    Both online, as well as the offline mode, is available for the ease of the individuals.

  • What if I missed any class?

    Our lectures are live. One can access the lecture from their home, workplace, or from anywhere.

  • I have 8+ years of experience and intend for a good salary hike, is it possible ?

    Absolutely yes, At CoEdify we have programs for 4+ years of experienced developers as well as for the 8+ ones for their higher salary possibilities.

  • Is there any entrance test?

    No, we dont take entrance tests.We evaluate the students to make a curriculum for them which best suits the individual's needs.

  • What is the fee structure?

    The fee will be decided after the counselling of the candidate.

  • What if I can not get the job?

    Getting you hired is our topmost priority, so not getting the job is not on the list. We refer our candidate to our partner MNCs and help them to crack their respective interviews.

  • How will I crack the interview?

    Interview preparations are a part of our curriculum itself. We have 1-1 mock interviews with our students to ensure they are well prepared for all interview rounds like technical, HR etc

  • How will my doubts get cleared?

    Doubt clearing sessions are a part of our curriculum itself. Also we encourage group discussions as we believe that we all can learn from our peers and fellow students.
